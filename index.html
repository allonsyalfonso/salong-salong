Ang Balangkas ng Salong-Salong

The Outline of Salong-Salong

Isla ng Salong-Salong is the living world of the Sina Una tabletop roleplaying campaign, a tropical archipelago steeped in pre-colonial myth, ancestral spirits, and the weight of choices that cannot be undone.

Isla ng Salong-Salong: Ang Sinilangan

The founding campaign of this world, now completed. A long adventure that ran for over a year, forging the legends, tragedies, and triumphs that now define the islands. The heroes of this campaign became the myths of the next.

Crafted and guided by DM Avips, this world was not written in advance. It was built at the table, shaped session by session, decision by decision, until the islands breathed with their own history, their own grief, and their own legends. What you find here is the record of that world: its places, its people, and the echoes of those who walked through it.

Isla ng Salong-Salong: Mga Nawawalang Salaysay

An active mini-campaign set in the same world, a few years after the original. New adventurers walk ground once walked by legends, uncovering the stories the first campaign left untold, and the truths that were buried with the fallen.
