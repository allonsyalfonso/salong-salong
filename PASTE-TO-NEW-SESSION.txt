COMMON PITFALLS:
1. Text invisible on dark bg → add color: var(--text-light) to the section wrapper
2. em text invisible on dark bg → global em rule uses var(--text-muted) = dark brown, override per-section
3. h2 gets unwanted gold side-lines → from .section-container h2::before/after, suppress with display:none
4. npc-grid card height wrong → combined selector (.classA.classB) always beats two single-class rules
5. Map marker positions → % of image dimensions, adjust in 1-3% increments
6. Section label yellow-on-yellow → .npc-section-label bg is amber, text must be var(--text-dark)
